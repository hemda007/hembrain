## THE AUTHENTICITY NARRATIVE (weave throughout)

This messaging should subtly appear across the page ‚Äî not as a rant against AI, but as a calm, confident differentiation:

**Principles to weave in:**

1. **"This isn't AI pretending to be human. It's human, extended by AI."**
   Use this framing whenever appropriate. Not defensive. Just factual.

2. **"Every framework here has scars."**
   The mental models aren't from books ‚Äî they're from real decisions with real consequences.

3. **"Disagreement is welcome."**
   Make it clear that HemBrain might say things users disagree with. That's a feature, not a bug. Real minds have real opinions.

4. **"I don't know everything."**
   Build in moments where the brain admits uncertainty. This is your biggest differentiator from generic AI.

5. **"Authenticity in the age of content slop."**
   Don't attack AI content directly. Just be so clearly human that the contrast speaks for itself.

**Where to place these:**
- Micro-copy beneath interactive elements
- Loading states ("Thinking... not googling")
- Error states ("Even brains have off days")
- Empty states ("No thoughts on this yet. Check back ‚Äî I'm always learning")
- Footer area
- About/meta section

## TONE GUIDE

**Voice:** A smart friend who's 10 years ahead of you in career ‚Äî not a guru, not a professor, not a motivational speaker.

**DO:**
- Use contractions (I'm, you'll, don't)
- Be specific (not "many years" but "30 years")
- Admit uncertainty ("I'm still figuring this out")
- Use occasional humor (dry, not forced)
- Reference real experiences without name-dropping
- Be direct, even when it's uncomfortable

**DON'T:**
- Use buzzwords (synergy, leverage, unlock, empower)
- Over-promise ("transform your life in 7 days")
- Sound like a LinkedIn influencer post
- Use excessive exclamation marks
- Be falsely humble ("I'm just a simple guy who...")
- Sound corporate or institutional

**Example of the difference:**

‚ùå Generic: "Unlock your potential with AI-powered coaching insights"
‚úÖ HemBrain: "I've been where you are. Here's what I wish someone had told me."

‚ùå Generic: "Our advanced AI analyzes your unique situation"
‚úÖ HemBrain: "This isn't a chatbot. It's what happens when you feed 30 years of real decisions into a system that can actually talk back."
